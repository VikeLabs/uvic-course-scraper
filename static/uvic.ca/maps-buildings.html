Campus emergencies

The Campus Security building has its own emergency power generator. It’s the university’s emergency operations centre in the event of a major emergency or natural disaster.

Get the UVic SafetyApp to receive emergency notiﬁcations from UVic on your mobile phone.

View the UVic campus evauation map in case of an earthquake or download a printable PDF evacuation map.
